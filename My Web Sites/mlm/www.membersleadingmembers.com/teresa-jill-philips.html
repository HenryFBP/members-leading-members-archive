Teresa "Jill" Philips

I am a proud union member and a dedicated tollway worker, keeping the union strong and the highway running safely for 33 years.

As a member organizer and a loud proud union member, I have fought for dignity and respect for Local 73 members at the toll way for more than three decades.

I believe that now, more than ever, we need leadership from rank and file members who know first-hand the struggles Local 73 Members face.

I am ready to put my heart and soul into building member power for the best representation possible, and to stand up to attacks on working people from Bosses and the Washington DC political class.
